We're a small but mighty team with Canadian roots, working on big things to make the

world a better place through our mission to Amplify Optimism.

In 2017, our CEO (Chief Executive Optimist) George Tsogas acquired Vancouver-based
Kit and Ace from its original founders, the Wilson family (previously of lululemon).

George was driven by a long-standing goal to lead an apparel brand — sparked by a
background in supply chain logistics, operations, and guest experience. Together with
business partner Melissa Kinnoch (CFO), he evolved the company's history of premium
design and quality construction to refocus the brand’s direction towards purpose-driven
apparel. In a market oversaturated by fast fashion, there was a clear appetite for
beautiful and highly versatile clothing that easily crosses seasons and goes from
indoors to outdoors, boardrooms to beach

Today at Kit and Ace, we are 100% committed to creating clothing that's Smart
(technical and functional), Easy to Love (big on comfort and style), and Made to Last
(timeless quality vs. fast and trendy). We ensure our clothes can stand the test of time,
and we know that when you feel good, you look your best.
Our core values of Optimism, Fun, Learning, and Responsibility are lived by our people
daily and are at the heart of a company culture that thrives on an ongoing supply of dad
jokes from George.

And this makes all of us here at Kit and Ace very, very proud.
