Kit and Ace from its original founders, the Wilson family (previously of lululemon).

background in supply chain logistics, operations, and guest experience. Together with
business partner Melissa Kinnoch (CFO), he evolved the company's history of premium
design and quality construction to refocus the brand’s direction towards purpose-driven
apparel. In a market oversaturated by fast fashion, there was a clear appetite for
